Welcome to PyStaff's documentation!
====================================

PyStaff fits model templates to optical and near-infrared spectra of nearby galaxies. These pages will give you a quick introduction on how it works. It's MIT-licensed, been used most recently in the paper `Vaughan et al 2018b <#>`_ and is being actively updated and developed on `Github <https://github.com/samvaughan/PyStaff>`_.
